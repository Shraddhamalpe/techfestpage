Information About TechFest 2025

TechFest 2025 is a premier technology festival bringing together innovators, developers, and enthusiasts to explore the latest in tech advancements. Engage in workshops, attend keynote sessions, and network with industry leaders.

EventList

Event Description Rules
Hackthon A 24-hour coding marathon where teams compete to build innovative solutions to real-world problems. Teams of 2-4 members. Bring your own laptops. No external help allowed during the event.
CodeNinja A coding competition testing programming skills in multiple languages through several rounds. Individual participation only. No cheating or plagiarism. Timed rounds.
HackAI An AI-focused challenge where participants build AI-driven applications to solve challenges. Teams of 2-3 members. Organizers provide datasets and tools. No external code reuse.
Quiz Competition A fun and engaging quiz testing knowledge of technology and innovation. Individual participation. No electronic devices allowed during quiz. Time-limited questions.
